Popular Questions
How do I verify my identity?
Go to Account Settings > Security and upload a clear photo of your Government ID. Our team will review it within 24 hours. Verification helps in building trust within the community.
What are the payment methods?
We support all major Credit/Debit cards, UPI, and Net Banking. You can also save your payment methods for faster checkouts in the 'Security & Finances' section.
How to post a property listing?
Click on the '+' icon or 'Add Listing' button in your dashboard, fill in the details like price, location, and photos, and hit submit. Your listing will go live after a quick review.
Is my data safe with you?
Yes, we use industry-standard bank-level encryption to protect your data. Your sensitive information is never shared with third parties without your consent.
